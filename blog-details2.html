Restoring Your Smile: Everything You Need to Know About Dental Implants in Kolkata

Missing teeth can affect more than just your smile; they impact your ability to enjoy your favorite food and your self-confidence. In 2026, Dental Implants have become the gold standard for tooth replacement in Kolkata.

Why Choose Implants Over Dentures?

While traditional dentures are an option, they often slip while talking or eating.
1. Durability: Implants are designed to last a lifetime.
2. Bone Health: They prevent the jawbone shrinkage that occurs after tooth loss.
3. Aesthetics: They look and feel exactly like your natural teeth.

The Dental Implant Procedure: Is it Painless?

Many patients in Kolkata hesitate due to fear of pain. With modern anesthesia and AI-guided implant surgery available at our clinic, the procedure is often less uncomfortable than a standard extraction.

Factors Affecting Dental Implant Cost in Kolkata

The cost varies based on:
1. Material: Titanium vs. Zirconia.
2. Technology: 3D CT Scans and Digital Mapping.
3. Brand: International brands vs. local alternatives.
