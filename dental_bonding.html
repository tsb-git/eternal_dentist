Dental Bonding Process Steps

1. Preparation: Clean and prepare tooth surface.
2. Etching: Apply acid to roughen surface.
3. Bonding Agent: Apply adhesive.
4. Composite Application: Apply and shape resin.
5. Curing: Hardened with light.
6. Shaping & Polishing: Smooth and match tooth.

Why Bonding?

  • Fix chips, cracks, or gaps.
  • Improve tooth shape or color.
  • Quick aesthetic fix.


1. Tooth prep: Clean and etch surface.
2. Apply resin: Shape and match tooth.
3. Hardened with light: Secure bond.
4. Polish: Smooth finish.
