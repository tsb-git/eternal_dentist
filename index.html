Hygiene & Sterilization


We practice austere sterilization protocols. Our clinic is equipped with an autoclave & also chemical sterilisation for heat-sensitive equipment. Thorough disinfection is performed after every dental procedure. U-V chamber for housing sterilised equipment. And last but not the least a state of the art Vacstation machine & two high end air purifiers to keep the ambient air free from viruses.

A root canal is a procedure used to save a tooth that is badly decayed or infected. Contrary to popular belief, a root canal doesn’t cause pain—it relieves it. With modern local anesthesia and rotary endodontics, the experience is similar to getting a standard filling.

A dental implant is a titanium post that acts like an artificial tooth root. It is surgically placed into your jawbone, where it fuses with the bone over a few months. Once stable, a custom crown is attached, giving you a replacement tooth that looks, feels, and functions like a natural one.
