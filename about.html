Years of Experience

Your Smile is the Signature of Your Confidence.

Eternal Smile Dental Clinic, true to its name, believes in retaining, upholding & embellishing your smiles. To us, keeping your teeth healthy & eternal is of paramount importance & we dedicate ourselves to that noble cause with fervent sincerity.

We are an authentic multi-speciality dental clinic where doctors of all leading specialities of dentistry are available. Our specialists are also among leading practioners in the city & attached to multiple reputed hospitals & educational institute.

Our principal consultant is Dr. Sudipto Bhowmik. He has been practising since 19years & was the recipient of multiple dental scholarships from the Indian Dental Association for his academic excellence. He is a Gold medallist & university topper. He has practiced in Apollo Gleneagles Hospital for 11 years in the capacity of Senior Resident Dentist & Department Administrator.



Hygiene & Sterilization


We practice austere sterilization protocols. Our clinic is equipped with an autoclave & also chemical sterilisation for heat-sensitive equipment. Thorough disinfection is performed after every dental procedure. U-V chamber for housing sterilised equipment. And last but not the least a state of the art Vacstation machine & two high end air purifiers to keep the ambient air free from viruses.
