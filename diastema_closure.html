Diastema Closure Process Steps

1. Consultation: Assess gap and plan.
2. Composite Bonding: Apply resin to close gap.
3. Shaping & Curing: Match tooth shape.
4. Polishing: Smooth finish.

Why Diastema Closure?

  • 1. Improve smile aesthetics.
  • 2. Enhance confidence.
  • 3. Prevent food trapping.
