Braces or Aligners? The Modern Guide to a Perfect Smile in Kolkata

Whether you are a college student at St. Xavier's or a professional in Sector V, a crooked smile shouldn't hold you back. Orthodontics has evolved, and you no longer have to settle for "metal mouth."

The Rise of Clear Aligners in Kolkata

Clear aligners (like Invisalign) have seen a massive surge in popularity. Why?
1. The "Invisible" Factor: No one knows you’re wearing them at a wedding or a job interview.
2. Food Freedom: Unlike braces, you can remove aligners to eat Kathi Rolls or Luchi without fear of breaking a bracket.
3. Hygiene: It’s easier to brush and floss, which is vital in preventing gum disease.

When Are Traditional Braces Better?

For complex cases involving severe bite issues or extreme overcrowding, traditional ceramic or metal braces might still be the faster, more effective route. Our orthodontists use 3D Digital Scanning to show you your "future smile" before you even start the treatment.

Smile Makeovers for the Festive Season

Preparing for Durga Puja or a winter wedding? A combination of aligners and professional teeth whitening in Kolkata can transform your look in just a few months.
